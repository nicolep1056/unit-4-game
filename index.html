Adventure Time Candy Game!

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.

You will find four Candy People of the Candy Kingdom below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number.

If your total score goes above the target number, you lose.

You can learn the value of each Candy Person by clicking on them.

Each time the game restarts, the values of each Candy Kingdom citizen will be reset, and there will be a new random target number to reach.

Use clues, math, and candy cunning to win the game!
